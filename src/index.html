WELCOME!

Book a stay at the spectacular Abiquiu Ranch on the Chama River

A gorgeous house perched above the Chama river on 22 stunning acres in the heart of Abiquiu. With gardens and fruit trees, an old growth "bosque," direct river access, and beautiful views, this is a magical spot for relaxation, renewal, and exploration any time of the year.

Accomodations

The House

The house marries old New Mexico charm--deep set windows, kiva fireplaces, antique and tribal furnishings, and original artworks--with modern comforts and conveniences throughout. The main section of the house is spaciously laid out with large and lovely living and dining room areas, a fully stocked kitchen, an adjacent game playing area, a wonderful screened sunroom, and a laundry and mud room.
