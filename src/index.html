Spectacular Abiquiu Ranch on the Chama River

Abiquiu, New Mexico

A gorgeous house perched above the Chama river on 22 stunning acres in the heart of Abiquiu. With gardens and fruit trees, an old growth "bosque," direct river access, and beautiful views, this is a magical spot for relaxation, renewal, and exploration any time of the year.

The Space

The house marries old New Mexico charm--deep set windows, kiva fireplaces, antique and tribal furnishings, and original artworks--with modern comforts and conveniences throughout. The main section of the house is spaciously laid out with large and lovely living and dining room areas, a fully stocked kitchen, an adjacent game playing area, a wonderful screened sunroom, and a laundry and mud room.

There are three large bedrooms in the main section of the house and two additional bedrooms in a second wing. The three bedrooms in the main section of the house each come with a private bathroom. Two of these bedrooms have king beds (convertible to 4 single beds upon request), the third bedroom has a queen bed. The second wing of the house is automatically included for groups of more than 6, or when specifically requested. It includes a bedroom with a king sized bed, a second bedroom with two single beds, a large shared bathroom, a common area with a wood-burning stove and queen size pull-out sofa bed, and two semi-private porch areas.
