Accomodations

There are three large bedrooms in the main section of the house and two additional bedrooms in a second wing. The three bedrooms in the main section of the house each come with a private bathroom. Two of these bedrooms have king beds (convertible to 4 single beds upon request), the third bedroom has a queen bed. The second wing of the house is automatically included for groups of more than 6, or when specifically requested. It includes a bedroom with a king sized bed, a second bedroom with two single beds, a large shared bathroom, a common area with a wood-burning stove and queen size pull-out sofa bed, and two semi-private porch areas.
