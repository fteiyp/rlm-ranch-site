Events

Wedding celebrations are welcomed all year round at Rancho Los Milagros. Both small and large gatherings can be held on this scenic farm estate. As a working horse ranch, the property boasts a rich character. Indoor and outdoor event spaces allow couples to have the best of both worlds. For the ceremony, they can enjoy the beautiful view of the Chalma River.
